Menstrual Calander

The menstrual cycle is a fundamental part of life for all women but it is a topic which often goes undiscussed. Many may have questions relating to the links between conception and the menstrual cycle, but be hesitant to ask their regular GP. Why are periods sometimes inconsistent? What can affect them? Is there are a time of the month where it is harder or easier to get pregnant? To help those who may be unsure, below we have supplied a helpful calendar guide to the monthly menstruation cycle, along with some answers to a number of regularly asked questions relating to menstruation and conception.
